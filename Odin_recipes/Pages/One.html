King's Cuisine I


Quadzilla Burger


Ingredients

  • 1 Teaspoon of Togarashi powder
  • 1/4 Cup (4 tbs) of Kewpie mayo
  • 1/4 Cup (4 tbs) of Japanese worcestershire sauce
  • 16 Ounce ((4 patties)) of 80/20 ground beef
  • 2 Teaspoons of Okonomi sauce
  • 1/2 Cup (8 tbs) of Caramelized onions
  • 3 ((Parmesan.American and Cheddar Jack cheese slices)) of Cheese slices

      • Directions

    1. Prepare the two buns on heat at a low temperature for 5 minutes
    2. Mix the Japanese worcestershire sauce and kewpie mayo together.
    3. Add the Togarashi powder to the sauce and mayo mix
    4. Fry the four patties and caremelized onions on heat for 12 minutes
    5. Place the cheese on the patties as they fry
    6. Place each patty on on the bread buns in succession
    7. Add the onions to the mix
    8. Pour the worcestershire sauce mix on the burger
    9. Add the final bun

      10. enjoy!
